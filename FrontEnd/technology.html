Technology Overview

VeriHarvest combines cutting-edge blockchain technology with IoT sensors to provide end-to-end food traceability and authentication.

Blockchain-Based Authentication

Our distributed ledger technology creates an immutable record of each food product's journey from farm to table, ensuring transparency and trust throughout the supply chain.

  • Tamper-proof transaction records
  • Real-time verification capabilities
  • Smart contracts for automated compliance
  • Decentralized data storage for security

IoT Sensor Network

Our advanced IoT sensors continuously monitor critical parameters throughout the supply chain, ensuring food products remain safe and within optimal conditions.

  • Temperature and humidity tracking
  • Geolocation monitoring
  • Contamination detection
  • Real-time alerts and notifications

Mobile Authentication

Our consumer-friendly mobile application allows anyone to instantly verify the authenticity and origin of food products with a simple scan.

  • QR code and NFC tag scanning
  • Product origin verification
  • Complete history transparency
  • Safety alerts and recall notifications

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about VeriHarvest's technology

How does blockchain ensure food safety?

Blockchain technology creates an immutable record of every transaction and condition change throughout the supply chain. This ensures that all data about a food product's journey is transparent and tamper-proof. If any safety issues arise, the exact source and affected batches can be quickly identified, allowing for targeted recalls and preventing widespread food safety crises.

What types of sensors are used in the IoT network?

Our IoT network utilizes a variety of sensors depending on the specific food products being monitored. These include temperature sensors, humidity sensors, light exposure sensors, accelerometers for shock detection, GPS for location tracking, ethylene gas sensors for ripeness monitoring, and specialized chemical sensors for detecting contaminants or spoilage indicators. All sensors are calibrated to food safety standards and designed for minimal power consumption.

How secure is the data on the blockchain?

VeriHarvest employs 256-bit encryption and secure key management protocols to ensure data security. Our permissioned blockchain network restricts access to authorized participants only, and all transactions are verified through consensus mechanisms. Additionally, sensitive commercial data remains private while still allowing for public verification of key supply chain events. Regular security audits and penetration testing ensure the continued integrity of our system.

Can the system integrate with existing ERP and inventory management systems?

Yes, VeriHarvest is designed for seamless integration with existing enterprise systems. Our comprehensive API framework allows for easy connection to ERP systems, inventory management platforms, and other supply chain management tools. We provide dedicated integration support and have pre-built connectors for popular systems like SAP, Oracle, and Microsoft Dynamics. This allows businesses to leverage their existing infrastructure while gaining the benefits of blockchain-based traceability.

How do consumers verify product authenticity?

Consumers can verify product authenticity using the VeriHarvest mobile app. By scanning the QR code or NFC tag on the product packaging, consumers instantly access the complete history of the product from farm to store. This includes information about the producer, harvest date, processing facilities, transportation conditions, and any certifications (like organic or fair trade). The app visually confirms authenticity with a green checkmark and alerts consumers if any issues are detected.
