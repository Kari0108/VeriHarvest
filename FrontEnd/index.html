Our Technology

VeriHarvest leverages cutting-edge technologies to ensure food authenticity and safety for consumers, suppliers, and regulators.

AI-Powered Fingerprinting

Advanced hyperspectral imaging and AI pattern recognition to create unique food fingerprints that detect adulteration and substitution.

Blockchain Traceability

Immutable blockchain records provide full transparency of food products from farm to table, ensuring authenticity and building consumer trust.

IoT Monitoring

Real-time monitoring of temperature, humidity, and storage conditions throughout the supply chain ensures product safety and quality.
