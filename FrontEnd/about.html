About VeriHarvest

Our mission is to revolutionize food safety and authentication through innovative technology.

Who We Are

VeriHarvest was founded in 2023 by a team of food scientists, blockchain experts, and AI specialists who shared a common vision: to create a world where consumers can trust the food they eat.

Today, we are a leading provider of food authentication technology, working with farmers, distributors, retailers, and regulatory bodies to ensure food safety and authenticity throughout the supply chain.

Our cutting-edge technology combines AI-powered fingerprinting, IoT monitoring, and blockchain traceability to create an unparalleled system for food authentication and safety.

Our Mission & Vision

We are dedicated to building trust and transparency in the global food supply chain.

Our Mission

To create a transparent and trustworthy global food system where consumers can make informed choices about the food they consume, while enabling food producers to protect their brands against counterfeit products.

Our Vision

A world where every food product can be instantly verified for authenticity and safety, empowering consumers with knowledge and confidence in their food choices while supporting ethical and sustainable food production.
